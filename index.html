    Sunny

    Sunny

    A sunny day is characterized by clear skies with minimal or no cloud cover. The sun shines brightly, and there's typically no precipitation. Temperatures may vary depending on the season and location, but sunny days often feel warmer due to direct sunlight. They are ideal for outdoor activities and often associated with bright, cheerful weather.

    Cloudy

    A cloudy day features overcast skies with little to no direct sunlight.

    Cloudy

    A cloudy day is characterized by skies covered with clouds, which can vary from light, wispy cirrus clouds to thick, overcast layers. The sun's light is diffused, resulting in a softer, more subdued brightness.

    Rainnig

    A rainy day has continuous precipitation with overcast skies.

    Rainnig

    A rainy day is marked by persistent precipitation, where rain falls continuously or intermittently throughout the day. Skies are typically overcast, with clouds covering the sun, leading to a dull and gray atmosphere. Rainfall can vary from light drizzles to heavy showers, affecting visibility and creating wet conditions.
